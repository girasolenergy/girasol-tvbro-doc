KanbanBro: Turn Any Web Page into Digital Signage

KanbanBro is a digital signage app that lets you easily display web content on screens in your shop. With just an Android device and a web browser, you can create dynamic in-store signage without expensive specialized systems. It's perfect for small businesses that want simple, flexible, and affordable signage.

Why Choose KanbanBro?

  • Use Any Web Content: If it can load in a web browser, you can showcase it with KanbanBro. This means you can display images, videos, websites, or even live dashboards – anything online becomes your signage content.
  • No Special Hardware Needed: Run KanbanBro on an Android TV in your store or a tablet on your counter – it will display your content in full-screen as a digital sign. Leverage devices you already have, from smart TVs to inexpensive Android boxes.
  • Always-On Reliability: KanbanBro is built for continuous 24/7 operation. It can automatically refresh pages, cycle through multiple tabs, restart itself periodically, and even recover from crashes to keep your screen content up and running all day.
  • Flexible Display Options: Supports both landscape and portrait orientations for your screen. You get a true full-screen view with no browser toolbars, so your signage looks clean and professional. An Android TV-friendly interface makes it easy to control without a mouse or keyboard.

Use Cases and Ideas

  • Promotional Image Slideshows: Continuously loop through banners, product photos, or menu pages. For example, show rotating images of daily specials or new arrivals to catch customers' eyes.
  • Video Playback: Play promotional videos or advertisements on repeat to engage and inform your audience.
  • Live Data Dashboards: Display real-time information like sales figures, inventory levels, or a live graph (e.g. updating stock prices or weather charts) for up-to-the-minute updates.
  • News & Updates Ticker: Reserve part of the screen for the latest news headlines or social media updates, automatically refreshed so the info stays current throughout the day.
  • Calendars & Announcements: Show an always-updated calendar of events, schedules, or announcements so customers and staff are informed at a glance.

Whether you run a café, retail boutique, or small office, you can tailor KanbanBro to your needs – from a simple digital poster to an interactive information screen.
