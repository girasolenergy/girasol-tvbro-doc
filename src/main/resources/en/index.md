You can open and close the menu bar with the Back button.

### Menu Bar

You can perform various actions from the buttons.

![menu_bar.png](menu_bar.png)

- Close button: Closes the app.
- Microphone button: Starts the microphone.
- History button: Opens the browsing history of pages.
- Bookmark button: Opens the bookmarks menu.
- Download history button: Opens the download history.
- Incognito mode button: Restarts the app in incognito mode.
- Settings button: Opens the settings screen.

### Address Bar

It displays the address of the currently open tab. Here, you can enter a website address or perform a search.

### Tab Bar

Displays a list of the currently open tabs.

When you switch tabs, the content of the previously open tab is unloaded.

Use the + button at the right end to open a new tab.

![tab_bar.png](tab_bar.png)

---

Long press a tab to open the tab context menu.

![tab_context_menu.png](tab_context_menu.png)

### Bottom Buttons Bar

![bottom_buttons_bar.png](bottom_buttons_bar.png)

- Close button: Closes the currently open tab.
- Back button: Navigates to the previous page.
- Forward button: Navigates to the next page.
- Update button: Reloads the page.
- Ad block button: Toggles the ad blocker enabled state.
- Popup block button: Configures popup blocking.
- Home button: Displays the home page.
